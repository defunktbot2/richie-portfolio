Basic Webpage

Background

In ART 221, we built a basic webpage to tell a personal story. I shared my grandma's miki, a savory Filipino noodle soup recipe. My go-to color palette is the complementary colors blue and orange. I selected the subject of images of the miki in a pot and a can of quail eggs first and then made a clipping mask in Adobe Photoshop. I also used Image Trace in Adobe Illustrator to vectorize the images. Finally, I coded the website in HTML and CSS using Visual Studio Code. I used bulleted and numbered lists to organize the ingredients and instructions.

Visit the website here: https://pueodigital.com/lcc/art221/s2022/richiel/miki.html.

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator (Ai), Photoshop (Ps), and Visual Studio Code.

Firefox Redesign

Background

Again, in ART 221, our final project was redesigning a company's brand. I chose to redesign the web browser Firefox. We had to sketch logotypes, letter marks, symbols, and character icons. I selected the symbol with the fox head in front of a globe, reminiscent of their old logo with the fox's body, but replaced with the head instead. I also went with an orange-to-red gradient for the colors. I picked Insaniburger With Cheese, a big, bold font for emphasis, and paired it with Inter for the body. Additionally, we had to create patterns, email ads, and mockups.

View the complete design brand style guide here: https://indd.adobe.com/view/a11fd6d9-9b4f-4e4e-b73e-3412ed2aabdb. Here is the full image of the sketches as well: https://photos.app.goo.gl/6RKQBhQuxfbKGtBV7.

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator (Ai) and InDesign (Id).

E-Commerce Rebrand

Background

In ART 229, I redesigned UBAE, an e-commerce website selling ube products, such as ube leche flan, cookies, and cheesecakes. I made the icons and changed their colors in Illustrator, but I mainly worked with InDesign for the layout, taking the image and text placement and eye flow into consideration. I also animated the dropdown menu and made the arrows clickable on the product photo slideshows for added interaction. I picked a purple and beige palette, the same colors as an ube tart. I used a playful and fun font called Bubblegum Sans for the headers and ABeeZee for the body. Altogether, it is a friendly and welcoming vibe.

View the entire client documentation here: https://indd.adobe.com/view/a2e06a78-2861-44ea-982d-79e91d2c5643.

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator (Ai) and InDesign (Id).
